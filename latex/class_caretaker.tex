\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Manages the stored Mementos, acting as a save-\/game manager. 


\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item The \doxylink{class_caretaker}{Caretaker} is responsible for keeping the \doxylink{class_memento}{Memento} objects safe, but it never operates on or examines the content of the Mementos. In this implementation, it manages a single "{}current"{} \doxylink{class_memento}{Memento} and handles its persistence by saving and loading it from a file. 
\end{DoxyItemize}
